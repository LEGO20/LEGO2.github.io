The LETSKO constructor is an evolution of the LEGO

LETSKO is a new 3D constructor that allows you to create anything you want! LETSKO is a designer series, which is a set of parts for assembling and modeling a variety of objects. You can attach another same cube to either side of the Letsko cube, since the designer has 3 directions for mounting (X, Y, Z). The LETSKO constructor is an evolution of the LEGO constructor! Now you can design whatever you want.

Application: From LETSKO you can collect such objects as buildings, sculptures, interior items. Everything that is built can then be disassembled, and the details used to create other objects.

Features: The LETSKO cube is the perfect size for the comfort of your child. LETSKO cubes can be assembled together and disassembled an unlimited number of times. The cubes are securely attached to each other with latches. The cubes can be rotated 90 degrees relative to each other and then assembled together. After assembly, the cubes are self-fixed. LETSKO embodies the idea of ​​modularity, clearly demonstrating to children how to solve some technical problems, and also instills the skills of assembling, disassembling and repairing equipment.

Benefits: 3D printing technology allowed the creation of the LETSKO designer, which was ahead of the competition due to its unique 3D modeling capabilities. Now with the LETSKO constructor, you are unlimited in design. You can increase the design not only up and down as in LEGO but in all directions. This is possible thanks to the new LETSKO dice manufacturing technology using 3D printing. The future has already come with the designer LETSKO.

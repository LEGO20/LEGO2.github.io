Medical face shield "Angel"

The visor is a natural barrier and prevents the drip transmission of coronavirus. Protects eyes and mouth from saliva, blood from talking and sneezing infected. The shield can also be used to protect the skin from small flying objects, paint, dust. Advantages of this type of visor:
+ Coronavirus is transmitted in 95% of cases by drip. The face shield is a natural barrier against the drip method of transmitting the virus.
+ The face shield "Angel" is convenient for prolonged wearing, as it is attached to the head with an adjustable fastener and does not cause any inconvenience to the doctor, unlike glasses. The optimal size allows you to easily turn your head and behave as naturally as without a shield.
+ The dashboard is adjustable in size and equally comfortable for both men and women.
+ The shield is made of nylon - a flexible material, and is more comfortable to wear with a regular cap.
+ The shield is easy to assemble without a special tool. For assembly, you need only 1 additional component - a lamination film, which can be purchased at the stationery store. The film mount is hidden inside for reliability and safety. Thanks to what the guard has a stylish appearance. The shield "can be assembled independently an unlimited number of times (change the film, adjust the size).

Additional Information
Before attaching to the shield, the transparent film must be punched with a hole punch at the points of attachment to the shield in accordance with the drawing. Also, for home use, going to the store and walking, it is allowed to use a stationery file instead of a film, which already has an attachment to the shield in the form of holes. To improve transparency, you can delete one layer of a file by trimming it along the outline with scissors.

Medical face mask "Angel"

Medical face mask "Angel".
The mask is a natural barrier prevents the drip from transmitting coronavirus. Protects eyes and mouth from saliva, blood from sneezing and prevents infection. The mask can also be used to protect the skin from small flying objects, paint, dust.

The advantages of this type of mask:

+ Coronavirus is transmitted in 95% of cases by drip. Face shield is a natural barrier against drip transmission of the virus.
+ The face mask "Angel" is convenient for prolonged wearing, as it is attached to the head with an adjustable fastener and does not cause any inconvenience to the doctor, unlike glasses.
+ The mask is adjustable in size and equally comfortable for both men and women.
+ The mask is made of nylon - a flexible material, and is comparable to a regular cap in terms of wearing comfort.
+ The mask is easy to assemble without a special tool. For assembly, you need only 1 additional component - a lamination film, which can be purchased at the stationery store. The film mount is hidden inside for reliability and safety. Thanks to what the mask has a stylish appearance. The mask "can be assembled independently an unlimited number of times (change the film, adjust the size).

Additional Information

Before application, the lamination film must be perforated with a hole punch at the points of attachment to the mask in accordance with the drawing. Film for lamination can be purchased at the stationery store. Also, for home use, going to the store and walking, it is allowed to use a stationery file instead of a film, which already has an attachment to the mask in the form of holes. To improve transparency, you can delete one layer of a file by trimming it along the outline with scissors.
